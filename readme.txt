Install a Facebook Fan Page widget for your wordpress blog.
== Description ==
Turn your visitors into fans of your Facebook page. BumpIn Facebook Fan Page widget fully integrates your page on Facebook with your website/blog. It not only increases the fans of your page but also the time spent by the visitors on your site and dcreases the bounce rate.
Following is the list of features of the plugin.

Users can become fans in just one click without leaving your website.

Full fan page stream including all the wall posts and user comments.

Official and Fan messages can be viewed in separate tabs.

Photos of featured fans.

Number of fans.

Post full messages to your Fan Page wall.


== Installation ==

Follow the procedure to install Facebook Fan Page for you blog/site.

1. Download facebook-fan-page.zip
2. Unzip facebook-fan-page.zip and upload `BumpIn_Facebook_Fan_Page.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
3. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
4. Drag Drop the Facebook Fan Page widget to your sidebar according to your need.(goto *Presentation-> widgets*)
5. Customize and Register your widget(FREE!) by visiting socialbar.BumpIn.com (If you have not already registered)
6. Paste the code you got from BumpIn website in the text box and click the "Save Changes" button
7. Verify your email address used for registering at socialbar.BumpIn.com and set your password by clicking on the link sent to your email account
8. Re-customize your widget by logging in at socialbar.BumpIn.com using your email address as your login name
9. If you re-customize the widget from the SocialBar admin panel, the changes would automatically be reflected on your site/blog
10. If you forgot your password you can reset it from http://socialbar.bumpin.com/site/forgot_password/ by verifying your email id
